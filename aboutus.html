Vision

NAMetrics is established to advance and promote the research, development, and validation of innovative New Approach Methodologies (NAMs) by fostering collaboration between quantitative and analytical pharmacologists in the pursuit of advancing drug discovery and development.

Team

Krina Mehta, PhD

Founder

Krina is a quantitative pharmacologist with over 15 years of experience. Her work specializes in pharmacometrics and translational pharmacology, currently serving as Director at Kyowa Kirin Inc., where she leads strategy and modeling efforts across various therapeutic areas including oncology and rare diseases. Krina has deep expertise in modeling approaches such as PopPK, PBPK, QSP, and semi-mechanistic modeling. She holds a PhD in Quantitative Pharmacology, along with an MS in Pharmacometrics and a BPharm. Her work is reflected in several peer-reviewed publications and conference presentations, and she actively serves on several professional communities, like ISOP and ASCPT. Krina is especially passionate about modernizing drug discovery and development while reducing animal use in pharmacology evaluations.
